# E-commerce Revenue & Customer Analysis (Olist Dataset)

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing the Olist e-commerce dataset to extract business and revenue insights using SQL and Google BigQuery.

The analysis aims to understand sales performance, product categories, regional differences, and customer behavior patterns.

## Tools Used
- Google BigQuery
- SQL

## Key Business Questions
- Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
- How do revenues evolve over time (yearly and monthly trends)?
- Which regions and states contribute most to total revenue?
- Are there regional differences in customer purchasing behavior?

## Key Insights
- Revenue increased steadily from 2016 to 2018, showing strong platform growth.
- A small number of product categories contribute a large share of total revenue.
- Sales and sellers are highly concentrated in major cities, especially São Paulo.
- Customer behavior differs significantly across regions.

## Business Recommendations
- Strengthen focus on high-performing product categories.
- Invest in marketing and logistics in high-potential regions outside São Paulo.
- Encourage seller acquisition in smaller cities to increase geographic diversity.

## Dataset
Public Olist Brazilian E-commerce Dataset.
